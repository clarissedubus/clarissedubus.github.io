Clarisse Dubus is a multi-disciplinary designer and interior architect offering comprehensive and personalised design services.

Upon receiving her Master’s Degree in Product and Environment Design, Clarisse worked with Barnaba Fornasetti on Fornasetti's first solo exhibition in France.

Since relocating to London, she has participated in several projects with local artisans, curators and artists. She previously worked at Gallery Libby Sellers and Dzek, a material-based design company founded by Design Curator Brent Dzekciorius.

Our studio helps clients with their residential and commercial projects, as well as offering consultancy services for interior and product design.
