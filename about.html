Clarisse Dubus is a London-based interior architect and designer.

She studied Design product in Dijon, France and Milan, Italy.

Since 2011, Clarisse has worked for the Italian Designer Fornasetti, the Gallery Libby Sellers and in collaboration with artisans and interior architect. She work for the design advisor and curator Brent Dzekciorius, and for the London brand Dzek.
