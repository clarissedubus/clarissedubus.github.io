Clarisse Dubus is a London-based interior architect and designer.

Upon receiving her Master’s Degree in Product and Environment Design, Clarisse collaborated with Barnaba Fornasetti on Fornasetti's first solo exhibition in France.

Since relocating to London, she has participated in several projects with local artisans, curators and artists. She previously worked at Gallery Libby Sellers, and is currently at Dzek, a material-based design company founded by Design Curator Brent Dzekciorius.
